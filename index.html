LevelOne was established in 1998 in Dortmund, Germany by Digital Data Communications GmbH.
By providing quality networking products and solutions, we've grown steadily throughout the years with Branch Offices in 20 countries around the world.

The internet has become a deeply integral part of both business and home life. Years of experience has taught us that the only way to stay on top of the latest technology and trends is by listening carefully to what the customer says.  

Further, LevelOne guarantees our pre- and after-sales support. We provide qualified advice on all professional networking planning, deployment and maintenance while providing detailed advice and answers to all inquiries from our consumer based products.

Digital Data Communications GmbH also carries 2 well known brands which are Equip and Conceptronic.
